==========================================
LEAVE & ATTENDANCE POLICY
Version: 3.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
==========================================

SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICIES
----------------------------------------

1.1 ANNUAL LEAVE (EARNED LEAVE)
- Entitlement: 20 days per calendar year
- Accrual: 1.67 days per month
- Maximum accumulation: 45 days
- Encashment: Up to 15 days per year at basic salary
- Notice period: 2 weeks for leaves exceeding 5 days

1.2 SICK LEAVE
- Entitlement: 12 days per calendar year
- Documentation: Medical certificate for 3+ consecutive days
- Family sick leave: 3 days per year for immediate family
- COVID-19: Special 7-day quarantine leave
- Chronic illness: Up to 30 days unpaid after exhausting sick leave

1.3 CASUAL LEAVE
- Entitlement: 8 days per calendar year
- Purpose: Personal emergencies, appointments
- Approval: Same day intimation acceptable
- Carry forward: Not allowed
- Minimum notice: 24 hours for planned leave

1.4 MATERNITY LEAVE
- Duration: 26 weeks (6 months) paid leave
- Eligibility: Completed 80 working days in preceding 12 months
- Commission: 6 weeks before expected delivery
- Post-natal: 20 weeks after delivery
- Flexible return: Work from home for 3 months post leave

1.5 PATERNITY LEAVE
- Duration: 15 days paid leave
- Eligibility: Within 6 months of child birth
- Adoption leave: 15 days for adoption of child below 1 year
- Single parent: Additional 5 days
- Surrogacy: 15 days leave applicable

1.6 OTHER LEAVE TYPES
- Marriage Leave: 5 days for self, 3 days for siblings
- Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family
- Study Leave: Up to 12 months unpaid for higher education
- Sabbatical: 3-12 months unpaid after 5 years service
- Jury Duty: Full pay with proof of summons

SECTION 2: ATTENDANCE TRACKING
----------------------------------------

2.1 WORK HOURS
- Office hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Monday-Friday)
- Grace period: 15 minutes allowed 3 times per month
- Late arrivals: 4+ occurrences = half-day leave deduction
- Early departure: Requires manager approval
- Weekend work: Prior approval required

2.2 BIOMETRIC ATTENDANCE
- Mandatory for all employees
- Marking: Both check-in and check-out required
- Location: Office premises only
- Manual correction: Within 24 hours via HR portal
- Data retention: 3 years for audit purposes

2.3 WORK FROM HOME POLICY
- Eligibility: After 6 months of employment
- Frequency: Maximum 8 days per month
- Approval: Manager approval required 48 hours in advance
- Equipment: Company laptop with VPN access
- Tracking: Online timesheet submission required

SECTION 3: HOLIDAYS & SHUTDOWN
----------------------------------------

3.1 PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
- Total: 10 national/regional holidays per year
- List published: December 15th for next calendar year
- Compensatory off: For working on declared holiday
- Festival holidays: 3 floating holidays per year
- Regional offices: Local holiday schedule applicable

3.2 COMPANY SHUTDOWN
- Year-end shutdown: December 25th to January 1st
- Annual maintenance: 3 days in June
- Counts against: Annual leave quota
- Mandatory leave: All employees must avail
- Emergency support: Rotational basis

SECTION 4: LEAVE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
----------------------------------------

4.1 LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
- Online portal: HRMS system mandatory
- Timeline: Minimum 24 hours for casual leave
- Advance notice: 15 days for long leave (>5 days)
- Approval workflow: Manager → Department Head → HR
- Auto-approval: Leaves less than 2 days (if balance available)

4.2 LEAVE CANCELLATION
- 7 days prior: No impact
- 2-6 days prior: Requires manager approval
- 24 hours prior: Medical certificate required
- No-show: Marked as absence without pay
- Repeated offense: Warning letter after 3 instances

SECTION 5: ABSENTEEISM
----------------------------------------

5.1 UNAUTHORIZED ABSENCE
- First occurrence: Written warning
- Second occurrence: Final warning with salary deduction
- Third occurrence: Show cause notice
- Continued absence: Termination after 8 consecutive days
- Notice period: Reduced to 15 days for habitual absenteeism

5.2 LEAVE WITHOUT PAY
- Applicable: After exhausting all leave balances
- Rate: Basic salary deduction per day
- Maximum: 15 days per calendar year
- Approval: Department Head + HR Head required
- Impact: May affect performance rating
