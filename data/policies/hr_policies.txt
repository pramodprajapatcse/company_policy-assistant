SECTION 1: EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
----------------------------------------

1.1 WORKING HOURS
- Standard working hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Lunch break: 1 hour (flexible between 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM)
- Core working hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (mandatory presence)
- Flexible work arrangements available upon manager approval
- Remote work policy: Maximum 2 days per week with prior approval

1.2 PROBATION PERIOD
- Duration: 6 months from date of joining
- Evaluation: Monthly reviews with manager
- Confirmation: Upon successful completion of probation
- Extension: May be extended by 3 months based on performance
- Termination: Either party may terminate with 1 week notice during probation

1.3 EMPLOYEE CLASSIFICATION
- Permanent Employees: Full-time with benefits
- Contract Employees: Fixed-term agreements
- Interns: Student trainees (max 6 months)
- Consultants: Project-based engagement
- Part-time: Less than 30 hours per week

SECTION 2: COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
----------------------------------------

2.1 SALARY STRUCTURE
- Salary disbursement: Last working day of each month
- Salary components: Basic (40%), HRA (30%), Allowances (20%), Variable (10%)
- Annual increment: April cycle based on performance rating
- Bonus: Performance bonus up to 15% of annual CTC
- Overtime: Not applicable for managerial positions

2.2 MEDICAL BENEFITS
- Health Insurance: Coverage up to ₹5,00,000 per family
- OPD coverage: ₹10,000 per annum
- Maternity benefits: As per Maternity Benefit Act 1961
- Annual health check-up: Free for employees above 40 years
- Telemedicine consultation: 24/7 available

2.3 RETIREMENT BENEFITS
- Provident Fund: 12% of basic salary (employer contribution)
- Gratuity: After 5 years of continuous service
- Pension: National Pension System (NPS) optional
- Superannuation: Company contributes 15% of basic

SECTION 3: PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
----------------------------------------

3.1 PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS
- Quarterly reviews with managers
- Annual appraisal cycle: January to December
- Self-assessment: Required before each review
- 360-degree feedback: Annual process
- Performance rating scale: 1 (Exceptional) to 5 (Needs Improvement)

3.2 PROMOTION POLICY
- Eligibility: Minimum 2 years in current role
- Criteria: Performance rating of 2 or better for 2 consecutive years
- Process: Manager nomination, HR review, Leadership approval
- Effective date: April 1st each year
- Salary revision: Up to 20% based on promotion level

3.3 RECOGNITION PROGRAMS
- Employee of the Month: ₹5,000 award + certificate
- Spot Awards: ₹2,500 for exceptional contribution
- Long Service Awards: 5, 10, 15, 20 years milestones
- Innovation Award: ₹25,000 for process improvement ideas
- Team Excellence Award: ₹50,000 for outstanding team performance

SECTION 4: EMPLOYEE RELATIONS
----------------------------------------

4.1 GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
- First level: Direct manager (within 3 days)
- Second level: HR Business Partner (within 7 days)
- Third level: HR Head (within 15 days)
- Escalation: Ethics Committee for unresolved issues
- Timeline: All grievances resolved within 21 working days

4.2 EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
- Confidential counseling services
- Financial planning assistance
- Legal consultation (pro bono)
- Wellness programs: Yoga, meditation sessions
- Mental health support: 24/7 helpline
