==========================================
TRAVEL & REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
Version: 4.3 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
==========================================

SECTION 1: TRAVEL AUTHORIZATION
----------------------------------------

1.1 TRAVEL APPROVAL MATRIX
- Domestic travel: Department Head approval
- International travel: BU Head + HR approval
- Emergency travel: Verbal approval followed by written within 24h
- Group travel (>5 persons): CFO approval
- Training/conference: Learning & Development approval

1.2 TRAVEL REQUEST PROCESS
- Submit request in T&E system minimum 7 days in advance
- International travel: 15 days advance notice
- Business purpose clearly stated
- Estimated cost breakdown required
- Pre-travel approval mandatory before booking

SECTION 2: DOMESTIC TRAVEL
----------------------------------------

2.1 AIR TRAVEL
- Economy class: Mandatory for all domestic flights
- Airlines: Lowest logical fare from approved carriers
- Booking: Through corporate travel agent preferred
- Cancellation: Flexible tickets for uncertain travel
- Airport transfers: Prepaid taxi or app-based cabs

2.2 RAIL TRAVEL
- Distance < 500 km: 2nd AC preferred
- Distance 500-1000 km: 3rd AC or 2nd AC
- Distance > 1000 km: 1st AC or flight as per cost
- Overnight travel: AC sleeper class
- Local trains: General class only

2.3 ROAD TRAVEL
- Personal vehicle: ₹8 per km (car), ₹4 per km (bike)
- Self-driven rental: ₹1,500 per day (small car)
- Taxi: App-based (Uber/Ola) standard category
- Company car: For senior management (VP and above)
- Carpooling: Encouraged for group travel

SECTION 3: INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL
----------------------------------------

3.1 FLIGHT BOOKING
- Economy class: Standard for all
- Premium Economy: For flights >8 hours with approval
- Business class: Only for C-suite or >12 hours flight
- Airlines: Preferred partner airlines only
- Advance booking: Minimum 14 days for best rates

3.2 ACCOMMODATION
- Standard hotels: 3-star or equivalent
- Business hotels: 4-star for client-facing locations
- Extended stays: Serviced apartments for >14 days
- Daily limit: $150-250 based on location
- Corporate rates: Must use negotiated rates

3.3 PER DIEM ALLOWANCE
- Tier 1 cities: $100/day (US/UK/Europe)
- Tier 2 cities: $75/day
- Developing countries: $50/day
- Breakfast: Included in hotel rate
- Pro-rata: For partial days based on 24-hour period

SECTION 4: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
----------------------------------------

4.1 ALLOWABLE EXPENSES
- Accommodation: Hotel room + taxes
- Meals: Reasonable restaurant charges
- Transportation: Taxi, public transport, car rental
- Communication: Phone calls, internet, roaming
- Miscellaneous: Visa fees, travel insurance

4.2 NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
- Personal entertainment (movies, spas)
- Mini-bar charges
- Laundry (except >5 days travel)
- Alcoholic beverages
- Parking/traffic fines
- Personal shopping

4.3 EXPENSE SUBMISSION
- Timeline: Within 7 days of travel completion
- Documentation: Original receipts mandatory
- Digital receipts: Acceptable with validation
- Currency conversion: Company exchange rate
- Split bills: Separate submission for personal items

SECTION 5: REIMBURSEMENT PROCESS
----------------------------------------

5.1 EXPENSE REPORT
- Create in T&E system with category allocation
- Upload all receipts in PDF format
- Provide business justification
- Attach approvals for exceptions
- Submit for manager review

5.2 APPROVAL WORKFLOW
- Manager: Within 48 hours
- Finance: Within 72 hours
- Compliance: Random audit for 10% claims
- Payment: Processed with monthly payroll
- Advance settlement: Within 30 days

5.3 TRAVEL ADVANCE
- Request: Minimum 7 days before travel
- Maximum: 80% of estimated expenses
- Settlement: Within 15 days post travel
- Outstanding advances: Salary deduction for overdue
- International travel: 100% advance for visa/airfare

SECTION 6: CORPORATE CARDS
----------------------------------------

6.1 CARD ELIGIBILITY
- Frequent travelers: >6 trips per year
- Senior management: Department Head and above
- New hires: After probation completion
- Limit: ₹2,00,000 - ₹10,00,000 based on role
- International: Separate USD card available

6.2 CARD USAGE
- Business expenses only
- Monthly statements required
- Personal charges not permitted
- Lost card: Report within 24 hours
- Misuse: Recovery from salary + disciplinary action

SECTION 7: COMPLIANCE & AUDIT
----------------------------------------

7.1 AUDIT MECHANISM
- Random audit: 10% of claims monthly
- Red flags: Round figures, high amounts
- Documentation gaps: Claim rejected
- Repeat offenders: 100% audit for 6 months
- Fraud: Summary dismissal + legal action

7.2 PENALTIES
- Late submission: 10% deduction per week
- Missing receipts: Claim disallowed
- Unapproved travel: 50% cost recovery
- Policy violation: Warning letter
- Repeat violation: Recovery with interest
